EECS 351 Final Project

Image Processing & Cross-stitch Patterns

Gabrielle Guarino, Seth Perrin, & Drew Hanley

An example cross-stitch pattern made using our program.
This cross-stitch had a height and width of 8 inches and a fabric count of 12.


Our cross-stitch generator takes in an image as input, the desired height and width of your cross-stitch, your fabric count, and the number of colors you want displayed. It outputs a cross-stitch pattern corresponding with these inputs.
